2025-12-14 Session 2 - Finding Footing

After having beat Kai senseless, the party deliberates on their current situation. Thalen stabilizes Kai, they drag the Arbiter Deacon stabbed into the kitchen, and the other two Arbiters—still alive but unconscious—are stuffed into a closet.

Once Kai comes to, he shares with the party that the imminence of the Origin Gem’s trade from the Deepcrystal Enclave to the Trinity Kingdom has resulted in the reallocation of multiple Prismatic Guard deployments. This shuffling of troops is why the communication outpost the party infiltrated was sorely unguarded; he had also received a anonymous tip to come to the bar the party is currently in if anything were to happen. He further elaborates there has been a string of attacks targeting Prismatic Guard facilities, and he’s personally worried news has gotten out about the trade and emboldened everyone with a lust for power.

Realizing they have no way to contact Jane and worried the fourth Arbiter that escaped will return with reinforcements, the party readies themselves to leave when they hear a knocking at the door. They are greeted by the unconscious body of the Arbiter that escaped and Heimdall. Heimdall inquires who knows the party’s faces leading to a decision to kill the three still-unconscious Arbiters and for Heimdall to take Kai—who’s too important to kill without incurring additional heat—to a Sapphire user specializing in memory manipulation. Soren executes the Arbiters, and the party renders Kai unconscious to carry him out.

As the party leaves, there is a knocking at the door and Heimdall urges the party they need to leave now. They don the helmets of the Arbiters or cleaning masks from the kitchen and exit through the rear entrance where they encounter Kade and Axel. The latter of the two questions the party on their appearances to which Thalen replies by decking Axel with a punch that brought him to death’s door. As additional Arbiters pour out onto the streets, Heimdall blinds all in the area with his Labradorite rapier and the party escapes into the Glowhollow.

As the party maneuvers through the Glowhollow streets, Heimdall touches on his personal history with Kai; the Jackals; and how Operation Hyena crippled the Jackals, other smuggling groups, and the Glowhollow as a whole years ago. Once the party arrives at the Jackals’s hideout, Heimdall and the party exchange information and conclude that Jane is likely aiming for the Origin Gem herself and they have all been led astray by her schemes. A deal is struck between Heimdall and the party—if the party helps restore the Glowhollow, they will have the full support of the Jackals in tracking down Jane and demanding reparations for being roped into a conflict of her making. Shortly after, Heimdall departs with Kai to find the aforementioned memory manipulation specialist.

Octave, Silver, and Doctor are all introduced to the party, and the Doctor brings them to his lab to care for the party’s wounds. They are given a chance to rest and engage Octave in conversation who elaborates on his role as a Quartz user and information gatherer for the Jackals. After a delightful talk with Thalen and Serra, he invites the party to an upcoming show he has in a few days. To kill time until Heimdall comes back, he and the Doctor suggest that the party should join Silver in investigating a rumor related to the trafficking of a new illicit substance in the Glowhollow.

After some rest, the party meets with Silver in the evening who exchanges few words with Thalen as they travel to a shipping port. Once there, the party discovers a group of thugs and eavesdrop on the conversation in which they learn the illicit substance is being distributed throughout the Glowhollow as a sort of trial run. They engage the group in combat but the dealer of the substance escapes in a literal flash of lightning. The remaining thugs all inject themselves and display an increase to their strength, speed, and durability. The party and Silver eliminate or otherwise incapacitate the enemies, and find one who hadn’t crystallized over from the substance’s side effects along with a still intact syringe. As Silver and Soren execute the survivors, Thalen notices the substance is a mixture of fine powder of multiple Gem types.

Upon return to the Jackals’s hideout, the party hands the syringe off to Doctor who speculates the powder likely has microscopic runes inscribed onto it implying the maker of this substance is well informed and equipped. He informs the party it will take a few days before he has any concrete results.

With their first full break since their meeting in the bar two days ago, the party rests and contemplates their next steps while they wait for Heimdall to return, Doctor to complete his analysis, and Octave’s concert to begin.
